Minnehaha Falls, a stunning 53-foot waterfall nestled in Minneapolis, Minnesota, is a tranquil gem surrounded by lush greenery and rich history. Its name, derived from the Dakota language, means 'laughing water,' perfectly capturing the falls' serene beauty. A must-visit for nature lovers and photographers alike.
